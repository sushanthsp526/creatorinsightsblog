## Outreach Email Best Practices That Apply to All Templates

### Subject Line Guidelines

**Do's**:

* Keep under 50 characters for mobile optimization
    
* Include the influencer's name when appropriate
    
* Be specific about the opportunity or ask
    
* Use emojis sparingly (1-2 maximum)
    

**Don'ts**:

* Use all caps or excessive punctuation
    
* Make false urgency claims
    
* Be vague or generic
    
* Use spam trigger words like "amazing opportunity"
    

### Email Structure Rules

**Opening**: Reference specific content within the last 2 weeks **Body**: Lead with value, be clear about what you're offering **Ask**: Make it easy to say yes with clear next steps **Closing**: Keep it warm but professional **P.S.**: Add personal touch or additional value

### Timing and Frequency

**Best sending times**:

* Tuesday-Thursday, 10am-2pm in their timezone
    
* Avoid Mondays (inbox overload) and Fridays (weekend mode)
    
* Research their posting schedule for clues about their active hours
    

**Follow-up schedule**:

* First follow-up: 5-7 days after initial email
    
* Second follow-up: 2 weeks after first follow-up
    
* Final follow-up: 1 month later (different angle/offer)
    

### Personalization That Actually Matters

**Shallow personalization** (everyone does this):

* "Love your content!"
    
* "Your feed is amazing!"
    
* Generic compliments
    

**Deep personalization** (sets you apart):

* Reference specific posts from the last 1-2 weeks
    
* Comment on their unique perspective or approach
    
* Mention specific details that show you actually consume their content
    
* Reference their personal interests or recent life updates (when appropriate)
    

### Response Rate Optimization

**Track these metrics**:

* Open rates by subject line type
    
* Response rates by template and personalization level
    
* Conversion rates from response to actual collaboration
    
* Long-term relationship success rates
    

**A/B testing opportunities**:

* Subject line styles (personal vs. professional)
    
* Email length (short vs. detailed)
    
* Call-to-action placement
    
* Personalization depth
