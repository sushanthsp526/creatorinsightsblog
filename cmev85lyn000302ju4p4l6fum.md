### Why Brands Love Niche Influencers

The influencer marketing industry is booming, projected to hit **$32.5 billion by 2025**\[1\]\[4\]. Brands of all sizes—from global retailers to indie startups—are prioritizing partnerships with micro- and nano-influencers because:

* **Higher engagement rates:** Micro-influencers (usually under 100k followers) see engagement rates of 5–20%, far outpacing the 1–3% typical of mega-influencers\[1\].
    
* **Authenticity and trust:** 82% of consumers are likely to follow a micro-influencer’s recommendation, highlighting the power of genuine, relatable content\[1\].
    
* **Cost-effective ROI:** Campaigns with niche influencers can deliver up to a 20:1 ROI, making them highly attractive to brands looking for measurable impact\[1\].

#### 2\. **Use Hashtags and Social Searches**

#### 3\. **Study Similar Creators**

* Don’t hesitate to reach out to brands that have recently collaborated with creators like you.
    

#### 4\. **Join Influencer Platforms and Networks**

* Partnership platforms like [impact.com/Creator](http://impact.com/Creator) and Influencity offer searchable databases and direct brand contact options\[5\]\[6\].
    
* Join online communities (Discord servers, Facebook groups) where creators and brands network and share opportunities\[5\].
    

#### 5\. **Pitch Proactively and Build Relationships**

* Feature brands you love in your content—often, brands notice organic advocates and reach out.
    
* Engage with brand accounts: comment, share, and DM with authentic interest\[5\].
